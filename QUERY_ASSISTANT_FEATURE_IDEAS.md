## üéØ High-Priority Feature Ideas

### 1. **Query Templates & Saved Searches**
**Description:** Allow users to save frequently used queries with their filter combinations
- Save query templates (e.g., "Bank of Uganda jobs - Last 24 hours")
- Quick access to saved searches
- One-click apply saved filter combinations
- **Use Case:** User frequently searches for "Bank of Uganda jobs" with same filters

### 2. **Export & Share Conversations**
**Description:** Export conversations as PDF, Markdown, or shareable links
- Export conversation as PDF with sources
- Export as Markdown for documentation
- Generate shareable link (read-only)
- Copy conversation to clipboard
- **Use Case:** Share research findings with team, save reports

### 3. **Follow-up Question Suggestions**
**Description:** AI suggests relevant follow-up questions based on the current response
- Show 3-5 suggested questions after each response
- Context-aware suggestions
- One-click to ask suggested question
- **Use Case:** Helps users explore topics deeper without typing

### 4. **Multi-Query Comparison**
**Description:** Compare answers from different queries side-by-side
- Run multiple queries simultaneously
- Compare results in split view
- Highlight differences
- **Use Case:** Compare "Bank of Uganda jobs" vs "Banking jobs in Uganda"

### 5. **Smart Alerts & Notifications**
**Description:** Set up alerts for new information matching saved queries
- Create alert: "Notify me when new Bank of Uganda jobs appear"
- Email/push notifications when new results found
- Daily/weekly digest of new information
- **Use Case:** Stay updated on specific topics automatically
